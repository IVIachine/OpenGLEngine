CONTROLS
Press Enter key to unlock and lock camera
Use mouse to look around(only when camera is unlocked)
WASD to moveQ and Z to move directly up and down
P to delete units (if applicable) and spawn 10 new ones randomly
F to have all units path to the target(floating dot)
Up and Down arrow keys to change target location
C to connect to server
M to invoke RPC (Hello, World!)
Press G to toggle debug mode
